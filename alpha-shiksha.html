Executive Summary

Alpha Shiksha approached Nivarcs to develop a centralized content management system that would allow them to digitize their curriculum, streamline content creation, and deliver a consistent learning experience across all their centers.

Our solution focused on creating an intuitive platform that empowers educators while providing powerful analytics to track student engagement and learning outcomes.

!

Content Management Challenges

Alpha Shiksha was struggling with paper-based curriculum management across multiple centers, leading to inconsistencies in teaching materials and difficulty in updating content. They needed a digital solution that could centralize their educational content while allowing for customization at individual centers.

!

Educator Experience

Teachers were spending excessive time creating and recreating similar learning materials. The client needed a way to provide educators with ready-to-use content while still allowing for personalization based on classroom needs.

!

Analytics & Insights

The client had no visibility into which teaching materials were most effective or how students were progressing. They needed robust analytics to measure engagement and learning outcomes to continuously improve their curriculum.

Our Solution

Centralized Content Repository

We developed a cloud-based content management system that serves as a single source of truth for all educational materials. Content can be tagged, categorized, and easily searched, making it simple for educators to find the resources they need.

Intuitive Content Creation

Our drag-and-drop editor allows non-technical staff to create engaging, multimedia-rich learning materials without any coding knowledge. Templates and reusable components speed up the content creation process.

Multi-device Compatibility

All content is automatically optimized for viewing on different devices, ensuring that learning materials look great whether viewed on classroom displays, tablets, or printed materials.

Discovery & Planning

We conducted workshops with educators and administrators to understand their workflow and identify pain points in the current system.

Design & Development

Our team designed an intuitive interface and developed the core CMS functionalities with a focus on ease of use and performance.

Testing & Refinement

We conducted usability testing with actual educators and refined the platform based on their feedback.

Deployment & Training

The system was rolled out to all centers with comprehensive training for staff to ensure smooth adoption.
